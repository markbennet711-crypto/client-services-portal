Frequently asked

No. This is not therapy. Messages are supportive, human-written prompts and reflections — not clinical treatment.

How private is my data?

Very private. We store data securely, don't sell it, and you can request deletion anytime.

What if I miss messages?

You can pause delivery and resume later. Messages accumulate and we tailor the next ones to where you are.
